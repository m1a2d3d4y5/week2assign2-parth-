CLASS RULES

  1. RULE 1 It's mandatory to attend classes, recordings are for revision not for first-time learning
  2. RULE 2 - Do join live classes from laptop/desktop, not from mobile.
  3. RULE 3 - Starting 10min and ending 10min of every class is for doubt session.
  4. RULE 4 - Feel free to ask tough questions to your instructor. In case you aren't asking questions in Live classes then either you are a champ or you aren't really learning.
  5. RULE 5 - Make sure you practice class questions after every class, watching video aren't going to help you to crack interviews.
  6. RULE 6 - Make sure you complete class assignments max by the end of the current week in which the class was conducted.
  7. RULE 7 - Do share your assignment on the channel and seek feedback from your instructor and peers.
  8. RULE 8 - Feel free to post any question on the channel.
  9. RULE 9 - Collaborate with each other by making friends/group/team in order to learn together and grow together at a faster pace. - Be ready to push yourself to bring out your best

    10. “Keep your habits right, keep your future bright.”
